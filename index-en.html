Hong Kong Award Scheme for Workers Serving Persons with Intellectual Disabilities 2025

Organized by The Hong Kong Association of Workers Serving Persons with Intellectual Disabilities Limited | Co-organized by Caritas Rehabilitation Service & Caritas Special Education Service | Sponsored by XXXXXX

Scheme Philosophy:

To promote the sustainable development of Hong Kong's intellectual disability services, we are organizing the "Hong Kong Award Scheme for Workers Serving Persons with Intellectual Disabilities". Through industry participation and recognition of outstanding staff, we aim to jointly enhance the quality of intellectual disability services in Hong Kong and achieve a harmonious society.

Objectives:

  • To recognize outstanding workers serving persons with intellectual disabilities in Hong Kong
  • To encourage more newcomers to join the intellectual disability service sector
  • To unite the industry and build a healthy, positive, and professional image

Awards: (Trophy + HK$1,000 Voucher)

For pre-school, school-age, and adult intellectual disability service organizations/units, the following categories and awards are available:

Professional Staff Category

Recognition Award: 5 recipients

New Talent Award: 2 recipients

Supporting Staff Category

Recognition Award: 5 recipients

New Talent Award: 2 recipients

Note: Professional Staff (e.g., Teachers/Social Workers/Therapists/Nurses/Psychologists)
Supporting Staff (e.g., Instructors/Welfare Workers/Health Workers/Assistants/Clerks/General Workers/Drivers/Cooks)

Additional: Nominators of award recipients will receive certificates of appreciation and vouchers

Assessment Criteria:

  • Knowledge & Skills: Appropriate application, quality focus, innovation, continuous learning
  • Service Attitude: Care for clients, dedication, responsive to needs, adaptability
  • Team Collaboration: Leading by example, active cooperation, effective communication, team cohesion
  • Personal Qualities: Professional dedication, contribution of strengths, optimistic attitude, positive thinking

Eligibility and Details:

  • Participants must be nominated by registered pre-school, school-age, or adult intellectual disability service organizations/units in Hong Kong
  • Nominees will first be screened by our Council, followed by preliminary selection by the "2025 Award Scheme Committee"
  • Shortlisted nominees will undergo final assessment (including interviews) by the "2025 Award Scheme Panel"
  • Panel members are professionals/senior scholars from the industry who will select awardees based on their outstanding performance/contributions in their professional or supporting roles

Recognition Award

  • Nominees must have 3 or more years of service in the same organization/unit as professional/supporting staff
  • Each service unit can nominate 1 person per category

New Talent Award

  • Nominees must have at least 1 year but less than 3 years of service in the same organization/unit as professional/supporting staff
  • Each service unit can nominate 1 person per category

Important Notes:

  • Nominees must be full-time employees of registered pre-school, school-age, or adult intellectual disability service organizations/units in Hong Kong
  • Nominees must attend the final assessment interview arranged by the "2025 Award Scheme Panel"
  • Award recipients must participate in media interviews during the event (subject to event arrangements)
  • Nominators cannot be nominees
  • The scheme does not have an appeal process; all decisions will be based on the assessment criteria/standards and final results

Note: To avoid conflicts of interest, Council members and their family members are not eligible to participate in this award scheme.

Nomination Period and Deadline:

Nominations open from May 1, 2025, until June 15, 2025 (midnight)

Nomination Method and Form:

  • Please use the QR code provided for the "2025 Award Scheme" to submit nominations
  • Nominators must complete and submit the nomination form before the deadline (submissions only accepted through the official QR code)

Scheme and Assessment Timeline:

June 15, 2025 (midnight) Nomination Deadline
June-July 2025 Initial Screening (Council)
July-August 2025 Preliminary Selection (2025 Award Scheme Committee)
August-October 2025 Final Assessment Interviews (2025 Award Scheme Panel)
